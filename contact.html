Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

Our average delivery time is 15-25 minutes depending on your location and restaurant preparation time. You can track your order in real-time through our app.

What if my order is wrong?

We apologize for any mistakes! Contact our support team immediately via chat or phone, and we'll make it right with a refund or replacement.

Can I schedule a delivery?

Yes! During checkout, select "Schedule Delivery" and choose your preferred time slot up to 7 days in advance.

How do I become a partner restaurant?

We're always looking for great restaurants! Fill out our partnership form or email partners@fooddash.com and our team will reach out within 48 hours.
